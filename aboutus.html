WHO WE ARE

Far Stafford, a private company created in 2019, headquartered in Williamsburg, Virginia. We are proud to be your businesses and careers development’ collaborator. We primarily provide client relationship management, business process outsourcing, professional outsourcing services and more.

We exist to make businesses thrive by helping them in reaching their objectives and exceeding their growth expectations. We collaborate with various organizations and agencies in the United states, notably the federal government, government contractors and businesses at all levels.

OUR MISSION

We strive to create an exceptional customer experience that has a positive impact on the people. By having a collaborative approach, we implement the necessary means and expertise to exceed our partners' expectations.

OUR VISION

Our vision relies on developing long-term collaborations with our partners. Strategy is the key, but its implementation requires time and effort, which is why we’re ready to fully invest in order to guarantee a monetizing and sustainable evolution for our collaborators.
